Welcome to Food Rescue Network

We connect wedding venues with NGOs to ensure leftover food is redistributed to those in need. Our mission is to minimize food waste and provide nourishment to both people and animals.

Our platform connects wedding venues with NGOs to manage and redistribute leftover food effectively. We aim to reduce food waste by ensuring that surplus food does not go to waste but instead reaches those who need it. By facilitating this connection, we provide nourishment to both people and animals, contributing to a more sustainable and compassionate society. Our mission is to make the process as seamless as possible, allowing wedding venues to easily partner with NGOs and ensure that leftover food is utilized for a good cause. This approach not only helps those in need but also supports environmental sustainability.
